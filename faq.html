How do I place an order for a handicraft item?

To place an order for a handicraft item, simply browse our online catalog and add the desired item(s) to your cart. Once you have finished selecting your items, proceed to the checkout page and provide your contact and payment information.

How long does it take to process an order?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. However, please note that processing times may vary depending on the item and quantity ordered.

Can I make a custom request for a specific item?

Yes, we offer custom orders for specific handicraft items. Please contact us with your request and we will work with you to create a personalized product that meets your needs.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy on all handicraft items purchased on our website. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us to initiate the return process and receive process and receive a full refund or exchange.

How can I contact your customer service team?

You can contact our customer service team by email or through our website's contact form. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-28 hours during business days.
